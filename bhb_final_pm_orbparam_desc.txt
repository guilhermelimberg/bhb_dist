The column names are the same as the main catalog, but we also include other quantities of interest. 

pmra		: proper motion in RA Gaia EDR3 (mas yr-1)
pmra_error	: proper motion in RA uncertainty Gaia EDR3 (mas yr-1)
pmdec		: proper motion in Dec Gaia EDR3 (mas yr-1)
pmdec_error	: proper motion in Dec uncertainty Gaia EDR3 (mas yr-1)

vr		: radial component of velocity in cylindrical frame (km s-1)
vphi		: azimuthal component of velocity (km s-1)
vz		: vertical component of velocity (km s-1)

jr		: radial action (kpc km s-1)
jphi		: azimuthal action, jphi = Lz (kpc km s-1)
jz		: vertical action (kpc km s-1)

energy		: total (kinetic+potential) energy (km2 s-2)
rperi		: pericentric distance (kpc)
rapo		: apocentric distance (kpc)
zmax		: maximum distance achieved from the Galactic plane (kpc)
ecc		: eccentricity, ecc = (rapo - rperi)/(rapo + rperi)

Lx		: x-component of angular momentum in Cartesian frame (kpc km s-1)
Ly		: y-component of angular momentum (kpc km s-1)
Lz		: z-component of angular momentum Lz = jphi (kpc km s-1)

xgal		: Galactocentric x position in Cartesian frame (kpc)
ygal		: Galactocentric y position (kpc)
zgal		: Galactocentric z position (kpc)

vx		: x-component of velocity in Cartesian frame (km s-1)
vy		: y-component of velocity (km s-1)
